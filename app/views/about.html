About Cuppa

Jennifer has a lot of tea. It takes up an entire small cabinet in her kitchen. With a couple overflow boxes next to the microwave. And yeah, there's a stash at work, too. And a couple bags she carries on her person most of the time.

Sigh. Let's just say that sometimes there's so much tea she forgets which ones she has, then they get old and that makes her sad.

There was little choice but to make this app or risk having wonderful teas go stale. Unacceptable.

Besides, she needed a cool project to learn Angular anyway.
